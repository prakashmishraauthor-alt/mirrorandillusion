Who Is Avadhoot Shivanand?

Avadhoot Shivanand is a contemporary spiritual teacher whose work centers on self-discovery, awareness and conscious living.

His teachings encourage seekers to move beyond conditioning, rediscover their Original Self and live with clarity, purpose and inner freedom.
