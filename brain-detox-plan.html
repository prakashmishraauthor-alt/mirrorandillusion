Why Your Brain Needs a Reset

Mental exhaustion isn't always obvious. Modern life floods the brain with notifications, information, stress, and constant stimulation. A reset begins by reducing what drains your attention and strengthening what restores it.

The 21-Day Brain Reset Challenge

DAYS 1–7

Awareness

  • ✓ No phone for first 30 minutes after waking
  • ✓ 10 minutes of silence
  • ✓ Drink water before caffeine
  • ✓ Journal one page daily
  • ✓ Sleep before 11 PM
DAYS 8–14

Recovery

  • ✓ 20-minute walk
  • ✓ 15-minute meditation
  • ✓ No doom scrolling
  • ✓ Read 10 pages of a book
  • ✓ Practice gratitude
DAYS 15–21

Renewal

  • ✓ Deep work session
  • ✓ Creative activity
  • ✓ Reflection journaling
  • ✓ Digital sunset after 9 PM
  • ✓ Weekly self-review
